About

Hi! I’m Kyra Lee, an illustrator with a love for visual storytelling through bold, character-focused art. I received a BA in Psychology with a minor in Digital Arts & Media from The University of Texas at Austin in 2025 and have illustrated for several special edition books throughout the past 3 years.

If I’m not drawing, I’m probably reading, running, becoming obsessed with a new piece of media, or hugging my cat.
